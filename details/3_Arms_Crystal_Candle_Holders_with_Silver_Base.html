Elegant 3-Arm Crystal Candle Holders

Combining beauty and functionality, these 3-arm crystal candle holders are ideal for adding a touch of sophistication to any room. Designed with a sleek arch shape and an antique gold finish, they bring a timeless elegance to your home decor. The solid nickel-plated metal construction ensures durability, while the K9 crystal adds a romantic sparkle when lit, making them perfect for creating a warm and inviting ambiance.

Measuring 16.1" in length, 7.3" in height, and 3.5" in width, these compact holders are designed to fit a variety of candles, including tea light and electric candles with a diameter less than 2.5". Whether you're looking to enhance your dining room, living room, or cabinet, these candle holders are a versatile addition to your decor collection.

Their stunning design makes them not only a great choice for home decor but also a thoughtful and elegant gift for weddings, parties, and special occasions. These candle holders are more than just functional—they are a statement piece that elevates the mood and style of any event.
